Building and floodplain source page

Use the local building office, not a generic answer, before rebuilding.

Permit requirements, floodplain administration, substantial damage review, inspections, and local higher standards can differ by municipality. Residents need the city/township/county source that actually governs their property.

Key contact Local building department.

The city, village, or township usually determines permit requirements and inspections for the specific property.

Floodplain contact Floodplain administrator.

If local contact is unclear, start with the local building office and EGLE floodplain resources.
