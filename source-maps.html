Map and data source page

Use the resident's location to source rain, elevation, flood maps, drains, and local GIS.

Macomb GIS and drain maps are a local example. Every Michigan county or community may have different GIS layers, public works records, drain commissioner maps, and local flood-history records.

Map rule Do not use one county map statewide.

Use FEMA and statewide/weather data for common layers, then swap local GIS and drain records based on the actual county or municipality.
