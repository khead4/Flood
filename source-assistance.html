Do not merge Water/sewer is not electric/gas.

Michigan and federal entry points

Statewide sources residents can start with

MI 211Statewide referral source for local food, housing, utility, disaster, health, and community services. MDHHS State Emergency ReliefMichigan emergency-relief program information for urgent needs such as heat, utilities, home repairs, relocation, or other eligible emergencies. Michigan Public Service Commission Energy AssistanceEnergy assistance and utility consumer information. MSHDA Housing ResourcesMichigan housing resources and programs that may connect residents to rental or housing support. MDHHS Food AssistanceState food assistance information for eligible residents. Michigan Child Development and CareState childcare assistance information. DisasterAssistance.govFederal disaster assistance portal when a qualifying disaster declaration or program is available.
