Evidence and source base

This prototype uses public guidance from government, engineering, and planning sources. These sources should be kept visible in the final product so users understand where recommendations come from.

FEMA NFIP Technical BulletinsGuidance for complying with NFIP building performance requirements, including flood openings and enclosures.
FEMA Homeowner’s Guide to Retrofitting / FEMA P-312Homeowner-facing retrofit strategies including elevation, relocation, wet floodproofing, dry floodproofing limits, and utility protection.
FEMA P-348 Protecting Building Utility Systems From Flood DamageTechnical guidance on elevating and protecting electrical, HVAC, plumbing, and mechanical systems.
ASCE 24-24 Flood Resistant Design and ConstructionMinimum flood-resistant design and construction requirements for structures subject to building code and floodplain regulations.
Michigan EGLE Floodplain Management GuidanceMichigan-specific guidance for local officials, substantial damage/improvement decisions, and state floodplain requirements.
Macomb County Hazard Mitigation PlanLocal hazard context including coastal flooding, stormwater systems, drainage issues, and repeated community flood problems.
EPA Green Infrastructure GuidanceStormwater strategies such as rain gardens, permeable pavement, planter boxes, and green roofs.

Important: This prototype is educational and UX-focused. Property owners should confirm requirements with their local floodplain administrator, building official, licensed architect, engineer, or surveyor before construction.
