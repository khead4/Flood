Evidence and Source Base

This platform uses public guidance from government, engineering, weather, mapping, and planning sources. Final decisions still belong to insurers, local officials, licensed professionals, and assistance agencies.

FEMA NFIP Technical BulletinsGuidance for NFIP building-performance requirements, including flood openings, enclosures, utilities, and flood-damage-resistant materials. FEMA Homeowner's Guide to Retrofitting / FEMA P-312Homeowner retrofit strategies including elevation, relocation, floodproofing limits, utility protection, and damage-reduction planning. FEMA P-348 Protecting Building Utility Systems From Flood DamageTechnical guidance for elevating and protecting electrical, HVAC, plumbing, fuel, and mechanical systems. ASCE 24 Flood Resistant Design and ConstructionFlood-resistant design and construction standard referenced by modern building codes for structures in flood hazard areas. Michigan EGLE Floodplain Management GuidanceMichigan floodplain-management guidance, state permitting context, and substantial damage/substantial improvement coordination. Macomb County Hazard MitigationLocal hazard context, mitigation planning, stormwater issues, repeated flooding concerns, and community resilience planning. EPA Green Infrastructure GuidanceStormwater strategies such as rain gardens, permeable pavement, planter boxes, green roofs, trees, and site drainage improvements. Open-Meteo Historical Weather APIHistorical precipitation data used for annual, seasonal, monthly, daily, and storm-event rainfall calculations by coordinate. Open-Meteo Elevation APICoordinate-based elevation lookup used to explain low points and where water may collect. FEMA Flood Map Service CenterOfficial flood map lookup for flood zones, map panels, and National Flood Hazard Layer context. Macomb County GIS LayersCounty GIS layer catalog for local map context and official spatial references. Macomb County Drain MapsCounty drain maps for drainage systems, drains, rivers, streams, and water-movement context.
